COVID-19 FORECASTER is a forecasting system that forecasts deaths, confirmed, and recovered cases caused by COVID-19 in Australia and its states.
Moreover, the system allows user to compare data from different states of Australia on various metrices.

COVID-19 FORECASTER features with a visual presentation of COVID-19 cases of the past and prediction for the next 10 days.
COVID-19 FORECASTER assists for deep analysis providing a comparison tool for comparing COVID-19 cases between different states of Australia.

Current version of COVID-19 FORECASTER features data from Australia and its states only.
